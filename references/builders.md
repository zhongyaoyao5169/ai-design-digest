## Sourcing rules

1. **Real posts only** — every Builder card must reference an actual recent X post from that account
2. **Specific date** — `time` field must be `X月X日`, never vague terms
3. **URL** — link to the specific post if known; otherwise the account's main page
4. **Substance over personality** — the card body should explain what they said and why it matters, not just praise the person
5. **Categorical balance** — across 3–6 builders, try to span at least 2 of the 4 categories above
